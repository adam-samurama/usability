Usability Study

Methods

What website did you study? Who is the target audience? What three tasks did you assign to your subjects?

We studied carrefour, the target audience are kenyans or foriegners living in Kenya that want to buy items from the shop. The three tasks I assigned include:

  1. Searching for specific items.
  2. Buying items using digital pay, credit, or debit cards
  3. Seeing my recently visted items
